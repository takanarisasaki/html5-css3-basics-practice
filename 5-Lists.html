There are two types of lists. of is ul-tags, or unordered list, and ol-tags, or ordered list.

ul-tags makes bulleted items, and ol-tags adds a number before the item name.

We have to specify in each item in the list that they are an item in the list. To do so, we use li-tags, or list tags.

Here is the unordered list:

  • Pizza
  • Sushi
  • Pasta
  • Curry
    •

    Here is the ordered list:

    1. Pizza
    2. Sushi
    3. Pasta
    4. Curry
